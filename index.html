Who can participate in the referral program?
Anyone can participate in the referral program.
What qualifies as a successful referral?
A referral is considered successful once the deal is completed, and the transaction is finalized.
Will I receive my referral reward in my bank account?
Yes, you will receive your referral reward directly in your bank account.
Is there a limit to how many people I can refer?
No, there is no limit to the number of people you can refer.
How do I track my referrals and rewards?
You can track all your referrals and rewards by visiting your profile via Login / Dashboard / Refer & Earn Tab.
What details do I need to provide for my referral?
You need to provide the name, contact number, and specific requirements of the person you are referring.
Can I refer buyers, sellers, or renters?
The referral program is only for buyers, not for sellers or renters.
What are the possible reasons I may not receive a reward?
You may not receive a reward if the customer is already a Realezi client, if the deal isn’t finalized successfully, if the deal doesn’t close within three months, or due to any vendor-related issues beyond Realezi's control.
When will I receive my reward?
You will receive your reward 90 days after the deal is successfully completed.

Realezi offers a personalized approach, acts as a bridge between buyers and sellers, provides end-to-end assistance, and maintains privacy. It also operates on a zero-brokerage model.

You can find residential, commercial properties, PGs, co-working spaces, and plots.
Why is trust a key factor for Realezi’s community?
Realezi operates on core values of trust, transparency, and technology, ensuring the right connections between people and properties.
How is the work culture at Realezi?
Realezi is committed to fulfilling promises and expects the same from everyone associated with the platform.
How does Realezi offer a hassle-free property search journey?
Realezi uses technology and experienced consultants to shortlist properties and services, making the search process smooth and efficient.
